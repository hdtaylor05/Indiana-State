Welcome to Evansville!

Welcome to Evansville — the cultural heart of Southern Indiana! Nestled on the Ohio River, Evansville brings together historic beauty, a strong sense of community, and exciting attractions.

Evansville is known for its warm hospitality, scenic waterfront, and active arts scene. It’s home to Mesker Park Zoo, the Evansville Museum, and the impressive USS LST Ship Memorial.

Downtown Evansville has recently seen major revitalization, with a lively dining and entertainment district. Major employers include healthcare systems and energy providers, and two local universities bring youthful energy and innovation to the region.
