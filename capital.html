Welcome to Indianapolis!

Welcome to Indianapolis — Indiana’s vibrant capital and cultural hub. From its iconic speedway to a thriving arts district and growing tech scene, Indy combines midwestern charm with metropolitan energy.

Known as the “Racing Capital of the World,” Indianapolis is home to the world-famous Indy 500 and the Indianapolis Motor Speedway. But beyond the track, the city thrives with a dynamic arts community, diverse food scene, and walkable downtown centered around Monument Circle.

Families love the Children’s Museum of Indianapolis — the largest in the world — and nature lovers enjoy the trails and green space of White River State Park. The economy is strong, supported by industries like healthcare, education, tech, and logistics.
