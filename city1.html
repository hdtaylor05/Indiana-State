Welcome to Fort Wayne!

Fort Wayne is Indiana’s second-largest city, blending historic roots with exciting new growth. From festivals to fountains, it's a city full of friendly people and surprising attractions.

Fort Wayne has made a name for itself through consistent revitalization and investment in its community. Downtown features the Botanical Conservatory, Promenade Park, and cozy cafés along the riverfront.

Visitors can explore Science Central, walk the trails along the St. Marys River, or attend the Three Rivers Festival, one of Indiana’s largest summer events. Fort Wayne is also home to professional sports teams and a growing arts and music scene.
